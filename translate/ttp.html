How to submit a TamperTubePlus translation:

-  Click here to download the translation file (3.5KB)
-  Open the file and follow the instructions included
-  After you're done, send me the file via E-Mail (sven.fehler@web.de)

Your translation will now be included in the next update and you will be credited in the script!

If you want to contribute in any other way, please feel free to also send me an E-Mail (sven.fehler@web.de)
